# Style Template: Research
## Scope: Research Projects and Academic Papers
**Goal**: Create a single, detailed README.md that emphasizes the project's contributions, methodology, and reproducibility for an academic audience.

### 1. Document Structure (Single File)
- **README.md**: The only file to be generated.

### 2. Content Requirements
- **Abstract/Summary**: A formal summary of the research objective and findings.
- **Key Contributions**: Bulleted list of the novel aspects or improvements introduced by this work.
- **Methodology**: Technical description of the approach, algorithms, or models used.
- **Reproducibility**: Clear, step-by-step instructions on how to replicate the results presented in the paper (environment setup, data preparation, execution).
- **Results**: Summary of the findings, including benchmarks or performance metrics.
- **Citation (BibTeX)**: If the user provided publication info, include a correctly formatted BibTeX entry for citing the work.

### 3. Style and Tone
- **Academic Language**: Use formal, precise, and objective terminology.
- **Technical Rigor**: Ensure descriptions of algorithms and data structures are accurate and use standard mathematical/computer science notations.
- **Evidence-Based**: Focus on what the data or results show.
- **Structured for Peers**: Assume the reader is a peer researcher with relevant background knowledge.
